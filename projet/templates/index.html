How to play

There are 2 players in the game, you are always player 1 in red. You start at the top left corner.

To win a game, you must own more squares than your opponent. To own a square, you must be the first to move onto it. A taken square cannot be taken back by the oppenent.

You can move with the arrow at the right or bottom of the board depending on the size of your screen size, or using directionnal arrow or WASD/ZQSD on your keyboard
